1. Zeppoli's Italian Restaurant & Wine Shop

Last but not least the best Italian place in Blacksburg is Zeppoli's Italian Restaurant & Wine Shop. This place is famous for its homemade pasta and sauces. As well as whole wine shop within the restaurant.

Negatives:

  • Little Expense

Positives:

  • Romantic atmosphere

  • Homemade Pasta

  • Wine

  • Low lighting

  • Quiet

  • Patio

Why is Zeppoli's Italian Restaurant & Wine Shop in First?

Zeppoli's Italian Restaurant & Wine Shop has the best gnocchi I have ever had. Even though that pasta is my favorite, everyone of their pastas are handmade with love and care and you can especially taste it. I believe this is the best Italian place in all of Blacksburg. But that's not the only reason this place got first. It was also because of it being a hidden gem behind a gas station and some other stores. It has low lighting throughout the whole store, which adds to the romantic atmosphere. Overall, I can't say anything negative about this place other than the fact that it is a little on the expensive side, but it is so worth it.
